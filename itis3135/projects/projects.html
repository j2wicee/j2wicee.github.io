1990s coupe — suspension refresh

Replaced worn shocks and end links, corrected a subtle rear sag, and re-torqued every critical fastener to spec. Road test confirmed quiet ride and neutral turn-in — owner uses the car as a weekend driver, not a trailer queen.

Daily SUV — underbody noise hunt

Customer reported a rhythmic knock over expansion joints. After isolating sway-bar hardware and verifying torque on control arms, we replaced a single worn bushing instead of an entire assembly. Noise gone, invoice stayed fair.

Sedan — parasitic draw overnight

Battery was borderline every morning. Rather than swapping parts, we measured quiescent draw, narrowed the circuit, and found a trunk lamp stuck on. Repair was simple; the value was knowing where to look first.

Hot-day A/C — weak vent temps

Verified pressures, inspected condenser airflow, and confirmed a tired compressor clutch was slipping under load. Replaced the compressor, pulled deep vacuum, and recharged to OEM spec. Vents now blow cold even in Carolina humidity.

Generic lean condition flagged multiple possibilities online. We smoke-tested the intake, verified fuel trims live, and traced a cracked boot post-MAF. Replaced the boot, cleared adaptations, and confirmed trims returned to healthy range on a long test drive.
